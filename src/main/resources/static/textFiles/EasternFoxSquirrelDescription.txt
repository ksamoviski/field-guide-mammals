Much  bulkier  than  the  gray  squirrel,  typically  weighing 1½ times as much. The fox squirrel’s body is a yellowish gray  with  a  reddish-yellow  belly,  cheeks,  and  feet.  Tufts  behind  the  ears  and  the  tips  of  its  tail  are  yellowish-brown.  The  tail  itself is a reddish-orange with a mixture of dark gray or black hairs throughout.
